Making a Lego Minifigure

Follow these steps to assemble your own custom minifigure:

  1. Gather parts: head, torso, legs, accessories.
  2. Attach legs to torso by snapping them together at the waist.
  3. Press the head onto the torso's neck stud until snug.
  4. Add any hats, hair pieces, or handheld items to customize.
  5. Enjoy your new minifigure!
